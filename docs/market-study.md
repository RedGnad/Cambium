# Cambium MRV - Market Study

This document keeps the broader regulatory and market context outside the README. It should be refreshed before any investor deck or legal/commercial claim.

## 1. Thesis

Agricultural evidence is becoming more valuable, but raw machine data is becoming harder to share.

Cambium sits between these two forces:

- buyers, auditors and finance teams need field-level evidence;
- farmers and machine operators need confidentiality;
- autonomous machines create strong evidence but expose proprietary behavior if logs are shared raw;
- blockchain fingerprinting is useful when it makes evidence tamper-evident without publishing sensitive data.

## 2. Why Now

### Green Claims

The European Commission states that 53% of green claims give vague, misleading or unfounded information and 40% have no supporting evidence. The Green Claims initiative is a clear signal that environmental claims are moving toward substantiation, verification and evidence trails.

Cambium relevance:
Cambium can produce upstream evidence packets that support substantiation without asking operators to reveal raw telemetry.

Source:
https://environment.ec.europa.eu/topics/circular-economy-topics/green-claims_en

### EUDR

Cambium is not an EUDR compliance product.

EUDR covers cattle, wood, cocoa, soy, palm oil, coffee, rubber and derived products. The European Commission lists the current application dates as 30 December 2026 for large and medium operators, 30 June 2027 for micro and small operators, and 30 December 2026 for micro and small operators already covered by the EU Timber Regulation.

Cambium relevance:
Even when a crop is not directly in EUDR scope, the direction is clear: origin, plot-level traceability, evidence and auditability matter more. Cambium can support traceability and due-diligence workflows by producing field evidence packets, not by replacing EUDR legal processes.

Source:
https://environment.ec.europa.eu/topics/forests/deforestation/regulation-deforestation-free-products_en

### Data Act

The EU Data Act started applying on 12 September 2025. The Commission says business users of connected devices, including industrial machinery, can access, use and share raw data generated by those devices; the Commission also explicitly references agriculture as a sector where access to industrial-equipment data can improve operations.

Cambium relevance:
Connected agricultural machines are exactly the kind of environment where better data access and clear data boundaries matter. Cambium's privacy layer aligns with the idea that data should be usable without forcing uncontrolled disclosure.

Source:
https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/news/eu-data-act-gives-users-control-over-data-connected-devices

### CSRD / CSDDD

The EU has simplified sustainability reporting and due-diligence rules, but simplification does not remove the need for evidence.

The Council gave final green light on 24 February 2026 to simplify CSRD and CS3D. It narrowed CSRD thresholds to companies with more than 1,000 employees and above EUR 450 million net annual turnover, and narrowed CS3D to companies with more than 5,000 employees and above EUR 1.5 billion net turnover. It also states that companies will have to comply with the updated CS3D measures by July 2029.

Cambium relevance:
The scope is narrower, but in-scope companies still need better value-chain evidence. Cambium can provide upstream, privacy-preserving field records that help buyers or auditors reason about what happened, when and where.

Source:
https://www.consilium.europa.eu/en/press/press-releases/2026/02/24/council-signs-off-simplification-of-sustainability-reporting-and-due-diligence-requirements-to-boost-eu-competitiveness/

## 3. Data Sovereignty Problem

The core commercial problem is not "how do we store more farm data?"

The core problem is:

> How do we let a buyer, auditor or finance counterparty trust a field operation without forcing the machine operator to expose sensitive telemetry?

Autonomous-machine telemetry can expose:

- field location;
- exact path;
- yield;
- safety behavior;
- navigation strategy;
- vendor-specific file formats;
- calibration choices;
- operator routines.

Cambium turns this into:

- public packet;
- privacy transform report;
- canonical hash;
- signature;
- private evidence references;
- public verification page;
- PDF report.

## 5. Market Gap

Most MRV products assume a farmer or project developer shares data into the platform.

Cambium assumes the opposite:

> Raw machine data is too sensitive to expose.

That makes Cambium especially relevant for:

- autonomous agriculture;
- proprietary machine telemetry;
- supply-chain evidence;
- green claim substantiation;
- RWA due diligence;
- audit-ready field records.

## 6. Claim Boundary

Cambium does not make a company compliant with EUDR, CSRD, CSDDD, SBTi FLAG, GHG Protocol or carbon-credit methodologies.

Cambium produces upstream, privacy-preserving field evidence that can support due-diligence and audit workflows.
